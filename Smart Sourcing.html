About Us

SMART SOURCING INC. is a team of competent professionals having rich experience in Textile Industry.We have a strong supplier base and can source variety of the best quality textile products at very competitive prices. We are working selectively with mills which provide consistent quality round the year, timely shipments and best after sale service. We have built up a strong Quality Assurance team that goes to the mills and checks their quality process parameters at various stages during production and inspects the material at the time of dispatch to ensure acceptable quality levels as per customer's specific requirements. We have a worldwide network and promote various kinds of products of well reputed mills in down south and upnorth in different international markets like China, Hong Kong, Egypt, Chile, Peru, Venezuela, Columbia, Brazil, Argentina, Portugal, Spain, Tunisia, Bangladesh & Korea etc.The difference is quite visible once collaborated with us.
